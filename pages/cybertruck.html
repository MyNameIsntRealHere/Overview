Cybertruck

The cybertruck was an award for beta-players of Retail Tycoon 2. If you weren't a beta-player, you will not be able to buy this vehicle. See the table below for all this vehicles specs.

Cybertruck Specs
** You need to have an area of 3x[tiles needed] in front of your unloading dock for your delivery driver to be able to park. This is NOT needed when you don't have a delivery driver. *** The cybertruck is a reward for beta-players of RT2 and not buyable for other players.
