Alan's Autos

Alan's Autos, located on the road above the loading bay, is the place where you buy new vehicles for you or your delivery drivers. You can find an overview of all vehicles in the table below.

Vehicle overview
Delivery speed is the amount a delivery driver can deliver in one minute. This has no effect for when you are driving the vehicle yourself. * This vehicle requires a gamepass. Once you bought the needed gamepass you will get this vehicle for free at the start of your save. You can buy more at Alan's Autos for the prices listed in the table. *** The cybertruck is a reward for beta-players of RT2 and not buyable for other players.
