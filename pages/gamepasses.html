Gamepasses

There are a total of 6 gamepasses, ranging from a new vehicle to custom color palettes for your store. The overview below ranks the gamepasses from cheap to expensive, including the description from the Roblox store-page.

1. Custom Radio (R$149)

Allows you to create custom music playlists for your store using any of the hundreds of thousands of music tracks uploaded by Roblox. - https://create.roblox.com/marketplace/music

2. Custom Floors (R$149)

Allows you to create custom floor textures using any Roblox decal.

3. Custom Color Palettes (R$299)

Allows you to create your own color palettes for most buildable items in-game, using any RGB color and most materials.

4. Electric Semi (R$299)

This goes vroom real fast.

5. More Land (R$399)

Gives you the option to buy extra land for your store totaling 1344 tiles (60% more than default).

6. Military Clearance (R$499)

Allows you to sell a variety of military equipment including guns, ammo, and vehicles. Comes with one free tank and truck that you can drive around. Also barbed wire fences. (No, the tank doesn't have any ammo).
