Delivery Drivers

A delivery driver is one of the employees you can hire for your store. When you assign them a vehicle and give them a place to park, they will start bringing goods from the loading bay to your store.

Delivery statistics per vehicle
* When you have bought the gamepass, this vehicle will be available in every save for free. ** You need to have an area of 3x[tiles needed] in front of your unloading dock for your delivery driver to be able to park. This is NOT needed when you don't have a delivery driver.

You can hire delivery drivers and assign them their own vehicle in order to make the delivery of sellables to your store automatic.
