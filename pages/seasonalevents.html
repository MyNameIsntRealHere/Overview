Seasonal events

Seasonal events are annual events in Retail Tycoon 2. During each event you can earn special decoration items and you will be able to sell limited-time items for the duration of the event. There are a total of 3 annual returning events:

1. Valentine Event

During this event you can sell teddy bears, heart chocolats and candy bouquets. To get excess to decorations like teddy bears and hanging hearts, you will need to collect 50 candy hearts hidden around the map.

2. Halloween Event

During this event you can sell pumpkins. To get excess to decorations like cobwebs and jack o lanters, you will need to collect 50 pumpkins hidden around the map.

3. Christmas Event

During this event you can sell christmas trees. To get excess to decorations like presents and christmas trees, you will need to collect 50 presents hidden around the map.
